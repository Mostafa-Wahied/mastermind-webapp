Mastermind

Game rules:
  • Guess the 4-digit number set by the computer
  • The computer will give you feedback on correct digits and their positions
  • "Correct numbers" are digits that exist in your guess but are in the wrong position
  • "Correct locations" are digits that exist in your guess and are in the correct position
  • If you don't guess any correct digits, the computer will show '0 correct number(s) - 0 correct location(s)'
