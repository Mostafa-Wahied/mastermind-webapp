Welcome to Mastermind Code Breaker game!

Game rules:

  • The computer will set a number of 4 numbers, and it's up to you to crack the code!
  • After entering your four digits guess, the computer will let you know how many correct numbers and how many correct numbers in the correct locations you have
  • Correct numbers are numbers that exist in your guess and in the wrong position
  • Correct locations are numbers that exist in your guess and in the right position

    • P.S. If you didn't guess any correct numbers, the computer will show: '0 correct number(s) - 0 correct location(s)'
